HOW TO PLAY

  • 1. Lobby: The Host waits for players to join, then clicks Start.
  • 2. Turns: Players take turns. The current turn is highlighted in Green.
  • 3. Attack: Click an opponent's card or avatar to target them (Red Ring).
  • 4. Confirm: Click the big Check Button to fire.
  • 5. Dice: The game rolls a D20 for Attack vs Defense. High roll wins.
  • 6. Win: Last warrior standing wins the Straddle Royale!
